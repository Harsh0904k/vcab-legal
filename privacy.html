Vcab Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [01/10/2025]

Vcab (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Vcab app. By using Vcab, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

  1. Information We Collect

    a) Personal Information:

    • Name, email address, and account details (via Google Sign-In or registration).
    • Optional profile information you provide.

    b) Ride Information:

    • Pickup and drop locations.
    • Date and time of rides.
    • Car type and passenger count.
    • Rides you create, join, or leave.

    c) Technical Information:

    • Device information (model, OS version).
    • Usage data (app crashes, activity logs) for app improvement.

  2. How We Use Your Information

    We use your information to:

    • Provide the Vcab service (match rides, manage bookings).
    • Communicate with you regarding your rides.
    • Improve and maintain the app.
    • Ensure security and prevent abuse.

  3. How We Share Your Information

    • Other Users and Drivers: Your name, pickup, drop, and ride details may be visible to users you share rides with.
    • Service Providers: We may share information with Firebase and other third-party services that help us operate the app.
    • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

  4. Data Storage and Security

    Data is stored on Firebase Firestore in a secure environment.

    We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data.

    We cannot guarantee 100% security; use the app at your own risk.

  5. Your Choices

    • You can edit or delete your account through the app.
    • You can opt-out of receiving non-essential notifications in app settings.
    • Leaving a ride deletes your association with that ride but may remain in ride history as required.

  6. Children’s Privacy

    Vcab is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

  7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Vcab after changes constitutes acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
