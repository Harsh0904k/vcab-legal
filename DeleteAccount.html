How to Delete Your Account

If you want to delete your Vcab account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to harshpvt09@gmail.com with the subject line: Delete my Vcab account.
  2. Include your registered email address or phone number in the email so we can identify your account.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

Upon receiving your request, we will delete:

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone number).
  • Your ride history and booking data.
  • Any other personal information associated with your account.

Some data may be retained for legal or operational reasons, such as compliance with financial or safety regulations, but it will be anonymized and not linked to your account.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days.
