About Us

Welcome to iCreate – a student-driven initiative dedicated to turning ideas into impactful tech projects. We are a passionate group of innovators from VIT Bhopal, led by Harsh Kumar, working at the intersection of creativity and technology.

Our Mission

To empower students to bring their ideas to life by building affordable, practical, and real-world solutions through collaboration, innovation, and technical skill development.

What We Do

From shared cab booking apps and smart irrigation systems to home automation and AI-based health diagnostics, we work on diverse projects that solve real problems while helping members gain hands-on experience with emerging technologies.

Why iCreate?

We believe every idea deserves a chance to grow. At iCreate, students don’t just learn—they build, lead, and innovate. We provide the platform, guidance, and peer support to bring their projects to life.
