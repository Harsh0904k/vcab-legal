About Us

Welcome to Vcab, your reliable and convenient shared cab booking app. Our mission is to make daily commuting easier, more affordable, and eco-friendly by connecting passengers with similar routes.

Our Vision

To transform urban commuting by providing a safe, convenient, and cost-effective shared ride experience for everyone.

Our Features

  • Book shared cabs for your daily commute or occasional trips.
  • Match with other passengers going in the same direction to save on costs.
  • Select car type and number of passengers for a flexible ride experience.
  • Manage rides seamlessly through our My Rides and Booking sections.
  • Stay updated with driver assignments and ride details in real-time.
